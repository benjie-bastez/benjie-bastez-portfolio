Integrations & Workflows

Behind the Scenes (Marketing Automation Demo)

This section explains how the marketing automation demo works using connected tools. A lead is captured through a form, stored in a spreadsheet, then automation triggers follow-up actions.

1) Lead Capture — Google Forms

Customers submit their details (name, contact info, service needed) through an online form. Each submission becomes a new lead entry.

Capture Form Submission

2) Lead Storage — Google Sheets

All form responses are automatically saved in Google Sheets, acting as a simple CRM or lead database for tracking and follow-ups.

Storage Lead Database

3) Automation Trigger — Make.com

Make.com watches the spreadsheet for new rows. When a new lead is added, it can trigger actions like notifications and follow-up steps.

Automation Follow-up

Note: This demo is for portfolio and learning purposes only. No real client data is used.
